The Temasek Chorale was established in 1990 and now has just over 70 members.

![H3.jpg](https://temaseksec.moe.edu.sg/qql/slot/u187/CCA/H3.jpg)All members uphold a common goal to develop a lifelong love for singing and abide by the values of harmony in voices, unity in spirit, creativity in expression and perseverance in training. Through the last decade, the Temasek Chorale has taken part in school musicals, public concerts and achieved Distinction at the Singapore Youth Festival Arts Presentation.
