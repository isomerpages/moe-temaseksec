<p>Shooting at TMS has been one of the school’s key CCA program. Training
sessions are conducted at the school’s own air-conditioned automated shooting
range.</p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="s2.jpg" src="/images/s2.jpg">
</div>
<p>Our shooters compete annually in the National School Games Shooting Championship
and may also participate in invitational shoots organized by the National
University of Singapore (NUS) and the Nanyang Technological University
of Singapore (NTU). The team trains twice a week, but there will be additional
training sessions held during the period nearing competitions.</p>

<h3>DSA Trial details:</h3>
<p><strong>Trial period:</strong> Between 30 Jun 2025 to 4 Sep 2025
<br><strong>Venue:</strong> Temasek Secondary School Shooting Range</p>

<tr>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Applicants should be able to:</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Train twice a week, and participate
in additional training sessions nearing competitions.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Exercise team spirit by working
together to achieve common goals.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Be physically and medically
fit (preferably no existing medical conditions)</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
</td>
<td rowspan="1" colspan="1">
<p>Applicants should:&nbsp;</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Be in sports t-shirt, shorts/track pants,
spectacles (if any). No personal shooting gear is required/allowed. The
school will provide all necessary standard items for the trial assessment.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have a meal at least 2 hours before the
trial.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Have adequate hydration at least half an
hour before the trial.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Trials will assess on applicant’s basic
psychomotor skills in addition to actual firing of air pistol or rifle
by applicant.</p>
<p>The following 3 basic psychomotor skills</p>
<p>will be tested to assess the potential of the</p>
<p>applicant in relation to shooting :</p>
<p>•balance</p>
<p>•arm strength</p>
<p>•concentration</p>
