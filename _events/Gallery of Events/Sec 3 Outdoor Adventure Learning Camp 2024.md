<p>The Sec 3s Outdoor Adventure Learning Camp was an incredible experience
for everyone! Although sudden rain came and went, (with thankfully no lightning!),
the Sec 3s were still able to enjoy the three days and had an amazing time!
From orienteering to outdoor cooking, from rafting at Sembawang Beach to
paddling in a sit-on-top kayak, our Sec 3s not only walked away stronger
and more resilient, they also strengthened their bonds of friendship during
their camp! Well done!</p>
