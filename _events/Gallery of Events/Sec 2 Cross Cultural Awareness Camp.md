<p>The Secondary 2 students embarked on an exciting excursion that took them
to Tiong Bahru, where they explored charming cafes and vibrant markets.
Their journey continued to the Changi Chapel, reflecting on the historical
significance of the area. At the SEA Aquarium, they marveled at the diverse
marine life, while the Peranakan Museum enriched their understanding of
cultural heritage. To top off the adventure, the students enjoyed a delightful
picnic, creating lasting memories with their classmates.</p>
