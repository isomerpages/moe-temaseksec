<p>Our Secondary Two cohort attended Camp Culture 2024, a two-and-a-half
day non-residential camp focused on enhancing students’ appreciation of
Singapore’s multiculturalism and heritage. Students had a chance to embark
on a trail around the historical estate of Tiong Bahru and to visit the
Peranakan and the Changi Chapel Museums. They also had a chance to learn
about the lives of our migrant worker community through the Prejudice Trail.
A hands-on session involving the preparation and cooking of local delights
also gave students the chance to learn about Singapore’s culinary heritage.
After two days of learning, our Secondary Twos spent a day out at East
Coast Park where they enjoyed time of bonding and eating with their classmates
and Form teachers!</p>
