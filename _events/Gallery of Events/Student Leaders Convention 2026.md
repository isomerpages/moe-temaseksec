<p>On 6 March 2026, Temasek Secondary School hosted its Student Leaders Convention,
bringing together student leaders for a day of learning, reflection, and
camaraderie. The convention featured an inspiring keynote address by Dr
Mark Lim, CEO and Co-Founder of ALIENA Pte Ltd, who shared insights on
innovation, leadership, and making a positive impact. Students also participated
in sessions on Peer Support and problem solving, equipping them with practical
skills to better support their peers and lead with confidence. Adding energy
to the day, alumni organised a lively inter-house game that strengthened
teamwork and school spirit. The event concluded on a high note with vibrant
performances by the Student Leadership Groups and House Captains, celebrating
the passion and unity of Temasek’s student leaders.</p>
