<p>On 21 February 2025, Temasek Secondary School successfully organized an
exciting e-games, featuring popular games like Brawl Stars, Mobile Legends,
and Super Smash Bros! The competition brought together students from the
seven houses in a spirit of camaraderie and friendly rivalry! Participants
showcased impressive skills and the audience showed great enthusiasm! Everybody
had a great time!</p>
