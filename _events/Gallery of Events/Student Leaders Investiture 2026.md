<p>Temasek proudly marks its 25th Student Leaders’ Investiture!</p>
<p>We are excited to celebrate the appointment of our Secondary 2 and 3 student
leaders, newly invested across five leadership groups: Student Councillors,
Peer Support Leaders, Sports Captains, Class Managers, and Young Ambassadors,
alongside our House Captains and Executive Committee members.</p>
<p>We are also delighted to present the Alumni Leader of the Year Awards
across the five leadership groups. At the same time, we extend our sincere
appreciation to our outgoing student leaders, whose commitment and passion
have made a meaningful difference and inspired those around them.</p>
<p>Through their dedication and spirit, they have played a key role in building
a vibrant, inclusive, and supportive school community.</p>
