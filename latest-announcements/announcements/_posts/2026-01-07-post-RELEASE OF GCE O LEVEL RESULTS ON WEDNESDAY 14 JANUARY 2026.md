<p>Dear Students, Parents/ Guardians</p>
<p></p>
<p>The results of the 2025 GCE O-Level examination will be released on&nbsp;<strong>Wednesday, 14 January 2026, 2.00pm</strong>.
We congratulate your child/ward for completing this important milestone
and affirm you for your continuous support seeing them through their education
journey together with us.</p>
<p><strong>&nbsp;</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>Administrative Instructions on Results Release for School Candidates</strong>
</p>
<p>We encourage all students to return to TMS on 14 January to collect their
results. Parents/Guardians are welcome to join us but please note that
parking is not available within the school premises.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Reporting Time:&nbsp;<strong>1.30 pm</strong>
</p>
<p>Venue:&nbsp;<strong>School Hall</strong>
</p>
<p>Attire:&nbsp;<strong>School Uniform (TMS polo-T or PE T-shirt allowed)</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>All students must be properly groomed and attired. No wearing of multiple
ear or facial piercings.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Male Students: Short, neat, and
non-coloured hair with no facial hair.</p>
<p>·&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Female Students: Non-coloured hair
neatly tied up.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Proxy Collection</strong>
</p>
<p>For students who are unable to return to school on 14 January2026 to collect
their results, please inform their Form Teachers in advance. Students will
need to appoint a proxy to collect the physical copy of their results from
the school with the authorization letter. Proxies are required to produce
the relevant documents for the school’s verification when collecting the
results.</p>

more tips and resources for students and parents/ guardians.</p>

<br>Thank you for your continued support and partnership.</p>
