<h2>HISTORY</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="H1.jpg" src="/images/H1.jpg">
</div>
<p>The Temasek Chorale started in TMS in 1990 and now has close to 80 members.</p>
<h2>MISSION</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="H2.jpg" src="/images/H2.jpg">
</div>
<p>The members all uphold a common goal to develop a lifelong love for singing
and they abide by the values of harmony in voices, unity in spirit, creativity
in expression and perseverance in training.&nbsp;</p>
<h2>TRAINING</h2>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="H3.jpg" src="/images/H3.jpg">
</div>
<p>The training is held twice a week with additional sessions conducted nearer
to competitions and performances. The choir also attends choral exchanges
and workshops.&nbsp;</p>
<h2>OPPORTUNITIES</h2>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/2025_Choir_2.jpg">
</div>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Perform in school productions such as Once Upon A Time, as well as school
events like Speech Day and Open House.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Participate in the Singapore Youth Festival (SYF) Arts Presentation.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Perform in public concerts such as Voices of Singapore Festival.</p>
</li>
</ul>
